You’ll be amazed at the tranquil landscapes of the town of Sogokope, in the Volta Region, as you journey from the bustling heart of Accra. Its scenery unfolds with vibrant green fields, small villages, and glimpses of the shimmering Volta River. Approaching the lower bridge, the gentle hum of water greets you, mingling with the calls of distant birds.

Arriving at Sogakope Beach Resort and Spa,

a deep sense of peace takes hold. The architecture harmonises effortlessly with the landscape, as though it’s always belonged here. Warm smiles welcome you and the gentle murmur of water nearby invites you to pause, breathe and simply be.

The rooms are sanctuaries of comfort,

spacious and filled with light, with windows framing serene views of the vibrant landscape outside. Every detail feels considered: the refreshing cool of the air conditioning, a television, telephone, showers inspired by African elegance and beds so soft they promise nights of uninterrupted, blissful sleep.

You’ll also be charmed by the executive suites,

a quiet oasis, with large, glass doors opening to the riverside view. Natural light floods the room, inviting you to step out onto your private terrace and jacuzzi. There, you feel the wood beneath your feet as you look out onto the water, a view framed by lush greenery and the open sky above.

Every aspect of the resort feels purposeful.

Meandering garden paths guide you to the spa, where skilled hands ease away tension, leaving you feeling weightless and rejuvenated. Whether it’s the soft breeze over the river, the quiet of dawn, or the welcoming embrace of your room, everything here feels designed to hold you—offering rest, release and a rediscovery of self.
This isn’t just a place to stay; it’s a haven where you come home to yourself.
